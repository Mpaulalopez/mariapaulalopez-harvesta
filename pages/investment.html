Existing investments are tailored to individual client needs and are confidential in nature. We are active in various geographic areas across Australia. Our experience covers most agricultural crops and commodities including irrigated row cropping, horticulture, permanent plantings, dryland cropping, and livestock enterprises and financial markets for risk management.

Our involvement with irrigation means we are also active in temporary and permanent water markets in the major irrigation regions. We adapt our investments to capitalise on horizontal and vertical integration where appropriate, and we have a proven track record in both green-field development and crop conversions from annual crops to higher value permanent plantings.
